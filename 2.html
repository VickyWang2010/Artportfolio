About me

From a young age, I've been captivated by nature and the world around me — whether through a mathematical lens or by simply soaking in its beauty. This curiosity has driven me to find creative ways to capture and express my experiences. Art has always been a way for me to step into another world, a space where I find both inspiration and inner peace. Over time, my passion for art has deepened, and now, with a solid foundation, I'm eager to explore how I can weave different cultures into my work. Looking ahead, I want my art to go beyond personal expression and address global issues, sparking conversations that matter. I believe even small efforts can create ripples of change, and I hope my passion shines through in these pieces — each carrying its own story and message.
